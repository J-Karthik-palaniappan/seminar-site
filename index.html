INFER Seminar Series

The INFER (Information, Networks, Feedback, Estimation, and Randomness) Seminar Series presents talks in a pedagogical, tutorial format, with research topics explained in a clear and accessible manner. Each talk develops a central idea in sufficient detail so that participants can readily understand and take it with them. The seminars are primarily held online.
